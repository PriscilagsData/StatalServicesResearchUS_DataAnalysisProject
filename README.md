#### 🗄 Brief description
<sub>  2026 study of contract bidding opportunities for an investment advisory company in confidential private sector.</sub>

<sub>  Historical contract data is analyzed, including time trends, amounts, frequently contracted companies, and public entities requesting services.</sub>
<sub>  Opportunities are examined based on expiration dates, categorized by urgency, contract amounts, location, and publication dates.</sub>

<sub>  Data from csv, Excel, and Snowflake. Mockup design, KPI definition, data visualization. Power BI tailored to the client's needs to identify trends and valuable insights.</sub>

**Some data was modified for reasons of corporate confidentiality.**
